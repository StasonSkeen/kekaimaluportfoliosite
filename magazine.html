magazine spreads

Magazine spreads detailing the Hawaiian martial art of Lua

  • type & grid

    I began my design process by finding type that would thematically coincide with the idea of a culturally Hawaiian martial art. I found that Kabel and optima when used together give the article a Hawaiian feel. After coming to the correct type I played with the grid that would determine the placement of content within the document.

  • variation & design

    I went through multiple design styles with different images and different ways to place body copy. Some of the images I had chosen and collected to use in the magazine were stagnant and uninteresting when used along the canvas. Finding meaningful and powerful imagery to use alongside well designed typography would be the next phase of creating these magazine spreads.

  • cover & table of contents

    The cover for the magazine, "Melee!" a magazine that discusses fighting and martial arts has a cover that display Muay Thai in a way that subverts the normally aggresive idea of the fighting style with a compelling portrait of a fighter.
