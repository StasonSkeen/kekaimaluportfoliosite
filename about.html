kekaimalu

The Peaceful Sea

welina e kākou,

My name is Stason Kekaimalu Skeen, I was born and raised on the eastside of O‘ahu in Waimānalo. I am a graduate of Kapi‘olani Community College's New Media Arts Program with an associates degree in science.

My objective within the graphic design field is to be able to work in a collaborative environment to improve the visual landscape of Hawai‘i. Being able think about and apply the graphic design process to the landscape of Hawai‘i is my oppurtunity to bring visual purpose and meaning to my home, Hawai‘i.
