heilung ui

Designing a site for Heilung the music group creating "amplified history"

  • lo-fi design

    Designing lo-fi UX protoypes to get an understanding of what the Heilung website will look and be used was important to help understand how the User will be interacting with this website. Allowing for this exploration helps one make sure that the User does not have a bad or confusing experience when visiting the Heilung website.

  • ui design variations

    After mapping out the site and understanding the UX I begas designing the UI. I created different variations of the site to create a breadth of design styles in order to pin down which direction would best fit Heilung and their identity. I created one inspired by European minimalism design inspired by the music groups European origins and another was inspired by the bands aesthetic of nature and heritage. I chose to more forward with the design that matched the bands viking aesthetic the most which was inspired by the bands ties to nature and heritage.

  • home page

    The home page features a full width and height embedded video of one of Heilung's most interesting features, their live performances. The purpose of having this be the home page is that it puts at the forefront to the user that the most important thing to Heilung is the music.

  • about page

    The about page features a brief description of the band and their purpose to create music that "amplifies history" of their ancestors and heritage. The about section features a unique drop cap to add typographic texture. An image of one of vocalists, Maria, is placed alongside the about section since her image was the inspiration for the band's logo.

  • music page

    Following the idea of reflecting the groups ideals the webpage that features sites that sell their music. Below that music distribution section is the lyrics section that takes the user on a journey through Heilung's lyrics from the top of the sky to the dirt of the earth. This is to reflect the bands close ties to nature and the elements.

  • merchandise page

    The merchandise page is split into clothing merchandise and physical music copy merchandise. The tabs allow the user to sift between the two types of merchandise and uses strong imagery to help differentiate between the two.

  • contact page

    The contact page carries the aesthetic of a cold viking setting. The contact form is made minimalist, a design choice carried over from another design, this gets rid of any unecessary space and allows the background imagery to punch through carrying over the aesthetic of nature that the band surrounds itself with.
